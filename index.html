About

The short version

I'm a QA Engineer with over a decade of experience building quality into products from the ground up. At Planning Center, I was the first automation engineer — I didn't inherit a framework, I built one. Cypress, Playwright, CI/CD pipelines, the whole stack. Before that, I spent five years at Arise designing training programs that measurably moved certification rates.

What sets me apart isn't just the technical chops — it's the ability to see systems holistically. I've led teams of 75+, negotiated fee-free donation processing with Apple and Stripe, and presented at conferences. I find the gaps nobody else sees and build the bridges to close them.

Outside of work, I'm passionate about faith-based technology. I believe churches and nonprofits deserve the same quality of tooling that billion-dollar companies get. That conviction drives everything I build.
